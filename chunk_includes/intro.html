Thank you for participating in this linguistic experiment.

In this experiment conducted by the UiL OTS at Utrecht University we investigate how people read certain expressions. You will see the instructions for the experiment on the next page. This research has been approved by the Faculty Ethics assessment Committee Humanities (FEtC-H) at Utrecht University. You may decide to stop the experiment at any time and your participation is voluntary. However, you will only be compensated if you finish the experiment.

The data collected in this study are stored in an anonymized form on a Utrecht University server. The data will be kept there for 10 years and will only be accessible to co-researchers. By participating in this study you give us permission for doing so.

If you want to know more about this research, please contact the researchers via email at j.l.winkowski@uu.nl
